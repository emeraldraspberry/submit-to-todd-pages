Patch Notes 0.10.19

As a new week begins, so has another week of changes. Sleeping should not require everyone to logoff and sleep all at once, so Harbor will be added to help ease this issue. Lookout for better vertical traversal and global map viewing this week.

>> New Plugins!

BackPacks+

Community requested plugin that allows you to have on-the-go storage! Use /bpp help for more info!

Dynmap

A dynamic global map of the Minecraft overworld! Since this plugin is known to impact performance, Dynmap will undergo a trial period to gauge its viability. The Nether and End will not be supported to help reduce rendering costs.

EssentialsX

A plugin suite as a loving treat. Use /essentials for all available commands!

Harbor

Improves the sleeping experience to keep the Phantoms at bay!

LuckPerms

Just administration stuff for better permission controls.

SimpleElevators

Make vertical traversal a breeze with simple to make and quick to use elevators! Use /elevator [help] to get started with it!

Vault

Required dependency for EssentialsX, nothing more.

>> Plugin Changes!

ExperienceTome

Fixed permissions to use the Tome of Experience and its upgrades. Sorry!

mcMMO

New quality-of-life improvements and increased leveling for seldomly used skills. Some of these changes were undocumented for the past few days (please forgive me).

    General
  • Sounds upon incremental level up have been disabled
  • Mobs spawned from monster spawns now give experience for skills (unarmed, axes, swords..), but with 0.5 effectiveness
    Alchemy
  • Experience modifier divisor: 1.0 --> 0.25 (x4 gain)
    Archery
  • Experience modifier divisor: 1.0 --> 0.5 (x2 gain)
    Herbalism
  • Experience modifier divisor: 1.0 --> 0.25 (x4 gain)
    Repair
  • Experience modifier divisor: 1.0 --> 0.333 (~x3 gain)
    Taming
  • Experience modifier divisor: 1.0 --> 0.25 (x4 gain)

Patch Notes 0.10.15

With mcMMO, BetterEnchantments, and BuildersWand being recently implemented, there has been a concensus on what needs to be balanced. Sorry kiddos!

>> New Plugins!

ChestSort

Automatically sorts items in your chest and inventory!

ExperienceTome

Store your base experience onto the Tome of Experience for use at a later date!

>> Plugin Changes!

BetterEnchantments

Essences dropped way too frequently to the point of cluttering inventories, so they were quickly a nuisance. Increasing drop chances will help curb this problem while still being potentially awarding.

    Essences

    Drop chances are calculated by the formula of 1/n, so there will be a 0.5% chance of receiving a common essence upon breaking a block

  • Common drop chance: 20 --> 200
  • Uncommon drop chance: 40 --> 500
  • Rare drop chance: 75 --> 800
  • Epic drop chance: 120 --> 1000
  • Legendary drop chance: 200 --> 1200
  • Mythic drop chance: 500 --> 1250
  • Ascended drop chance: 10000 --> 1500

BuildersWand

The Builders Wand should at least be durable enough to last for its intended purpose as a project builder tool.

    Builders Wand
  • Item durability: 130 --> 390 (1/4 of a diamond tool)

mcMMO
